Fatal Mission: V643

Documenting the November 4, 1977 Incident....

On November 4, 1977, an Indian Air Force TU-124K was on a VIP Mission from Delhi to Jorhat. V643 crash-landed in a paddy field on final approach. The cockpit crew perished in the accident. Prime Minister Moraji Desai, his entourage, and the remaining crew survived.

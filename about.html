About Me

Born into the IAF family at the MH-Jullunder, Neale is indebted to his parents for his lifelong love for the Air Force and aviation. He is also indebted to his family for supporting his pursuit of his passions. He grew up on a number of air-bases in India and moved to, worked, and lives in the Pittsburgh Area. He loves family, friends, reading, nature, and the outdoors, and enjoys puttering. He is an avid aviator and has been compiling information and researching this incident since 1977. He is also the nephew and godson of the Late Wng. Cdr. Clarence D'Lima - Captain of the ill-fated V643. This website is at one intersection of his passion and interests.
